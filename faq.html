Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a Ponzi scheme?
No. We pay users from actual platform income — including registration fees, sponsored content, YouTube monetization, and affiliate partnerships. No withdrawals are allowed without task completion. Our value system makes the model sustainable.
Can I start at any Level, e.g 5 or Level 10?
Yes! You can choose any level on registration and earn according to that level’s rewards
How do I earn daily?
Just log in and watch one YouTube video daily. You'll can earn upto ₦300 and above instantly.
How much is the registration?
The entry fee for Level 1 is ₦15,000.
How much can I earn?
Earnings depend on your level, ranging from ₦300 daily at Level 1 to referral bonuses up to ₦768,000 at Level 10.
Can I upgrade to earn more?
Yes! You can upgrade to any level up to Level 10. Each level doubles your earning potential.
Is referral required?
No, it’s optional. But if you refer someone, you earn ₦3,000 (or more depending on their level).
How do upgrades work?
Upgrades are optional. If you choose to upgrade, you’ll pay double your current level fee and unlock higher daily earnings and referral bonuses.
How do I get paid?
Currently, earnings are withdrawn weekly to your bank or wallet, once verified.
